📘 eComApp – Full Project Outline

🧭 1. Who Are We

We are a next-generation multipurpose eCommerce platform combining:

Affordable global shopping
Mobile-first experience (Expo app)
Content + community-driven commerce

Our mission:
👉 “Make online shopping faster, cheaper, and more accessible for everyone.”

❗ 2. What is the Main Problem It Solves
Global Problems:
High product prices due to middlemen
Slow delivery times
Limited product variety in local markets
Poor mobile shopping experience in developing regions
Local (UK & similar markets):
Lack of trust in online shopping
Payment limitations
Poor logistics tracking

🛍️ 3. What Problem Our eCommerce Store Solves

👉 We solve 3 key pain points:

Affordability
Direct sourcing (like Shein/Temu model)
Accessibility
Mobile-first app (Expo-based)
Easy UI for all users
Trust & Convenience
COD + digital payments
Real-time tracking

⚙️ 4. How Our Store Solves This Problem

Direct supplier integration (reduce cost)
WordPress + WooCommerce backend for fast management
Mobile app (Expo) for better UX
Integrated payments (eSewa, Khalti, Stripe)
Smart logistics tracking system
